What Are Teeth Whitening?

Professional teeth whitening is a safe cosmetic dental treatment designed to remove stains and discoloration from your teeth. Over time, coffee, tea, smoking, aging, and certain foods can cause teeth to lose their natural brightness.

During treatment, a dentist applies a professional-grade whitening gel that gently breaks down deep stains without damaging enamel. A special light may be used to accelerate the whitening process, giving you noticeable results immediately after the session.

Unlike store-bought kits, professional whitening is clinically controlled, safer for gums, and produces faster, longer-lasting results.

Our Teeth Whitening Process — Step by Step

1
Smile Assessment & Shade Recording (Day 1)

Your dentist evaluates tooth health and records your current tooth shade to measure visible improvement after treatment.

2
Teeth Preparation & Protection

Gums and soft tissues are carefully protected to ensure a safe and comfortable whitening experience.

3
Application of Professional Whitening Gel (60 mins)

A high-quality whitening gel is applied to the teeth and activated using advanced technology to break down deep stains effectively.

4
Shade Enhancement & Final Polish

The whitening gel is removed, teeth are cleaned, and your new brighter shade is revealed instantly.

5
Post-Treatment Care Guidance

You receive personalized instructions to help maintain your whitening results for as long as possible.
