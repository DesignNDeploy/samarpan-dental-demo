What Are Braces & Orthodontics?

Misaligned, crowded, or uneven teeth can affect more than just your appearance — they can impact how you chew, speak, and maintain oral hygiene. Orthodontics is a specialized branch of dentistry focused on correcting the position of teeth and jaws to create a healthier, properly aligned smile.

Braces and clear aligners work by applying gentle, controlled pressure over time to gradually move teeth into their ideal position. Treatment may include traditional metal braces, tooth-colored ceramic braces, or nearly invisible clear aligners, depending on your needs and lifestyle.

Unlike cosmetic treatments that only improve appearance, orthodontic care enhances both function and aesthetics. Properly aligned teeth are easier to clean, reduce strain on the jaw, and help prevent long-term dental problems — giving you a confident smile that lasts for years.

Our Braces & Orthodontics Process — Step by Step

1
Consultation & Digital Smile Assessment (Day 1)

Our orthodontist evaluates your teeth alignment, bite, and jaw structure using digital scans and X-rays. A personalized treatment plan is created based on your needs, along with a clear timeline and cost estimate — completely obligation-free.

2
Braces Placement / Aligner Start (45–60 mins)

Braces are carefully placed on your teeth, or you receive your custom clear aligners. The procedure is comfortable and minimally invasive. You’ll also get guidance on cleaning, eating habits, and caring for your orthodontic appliance.

3
Alignment & Adjustment Phase (6–18 months)

Your teeth gradually move into their ideal position through gentle, controlled pressure. Periodic adjustment visits or aligner changes ensure steady progress while you continue your normal daily routine.

4
Retainer & Smile Protection (After Treatment)

Once alignment is complete, a custom retainer is provided to maintain your new smile and prevent teeth from shifting back — ensuring long-lasting results.
