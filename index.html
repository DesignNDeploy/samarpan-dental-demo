Who We Are

We Know Dahod Families — Because We're Part of One Too.

Samarpan Dental Clinic was founded with one clear purpose: to make world-class dental care available to every family in Dahod and the surrounding Panchmahal region — without the stress, cost, or time of traveling to Vadodara or Ahmedabad.

For over a decade, families from Dahod, Limkheda, Devgadh Baria, and beyond have walked through our doors with anxiety — and left with confidence. Our clinic is equipped with digital X-ray technology, sterilized single-use instruments, and air-conditioned treatment rooms designed to keep you calm and comfortable.

This is one of the most common concerns we hear from adults. We offer ceramic (tooth-coloured) braces and clear aligners specifically for this reason. Clear aligners are nearly invisible, removable for meals and important meetings, and very comfortable. Many of our adult patients have completed orthodontic treatment without most colleagues even noticing. Come in for a consultation and we'll show you options that suit your lifestyle and budget.
The entire implant process typically takes 3 to 6 months from first consultation to final crown placement. The implant placement itself is a 45–60 minute procedure under local anaesthesia. A healing period of 2–4 months follows where the implant fuses with the jawbone — during which you're given a temporary tooth. The final crown is then fitted. Most patients find the procedure far more straightforward than anticipated.
Every instrument at Samarpan is autoclave-sterilized after every single use. We use disposable needles, gloves, and suction tips that are never reused. Clinical areas are disinfected between patients. We follow sterilization and infection control guidelines set by the Dental Council of India. We're happy to walk you through our sterilization process if you'd like to see it in person.
